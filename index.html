Acquisition Criteria (Buy Box)

  • Lot size: 0.20–0.35 ac typical (10–15k sf); larger tracts welcome.
  • Access: Paved or reliable graded roads; recorded easements preferred.
  • Utilities: Electric at street preferred; city/well water & city/septic considered.
  • Environmental: Limited/mitigable flood or wetlands; FEMA/USFWS reviewed.
  • Zoning & comps: SFR-compatible, strong comps and price velocity.
  • Title & HOA: Clean title; workable restrictions/dues.

FAQs

How fast can you evaluate?

Usually within 24–48 hours after we receive APN/address and basic utility/access info.

Do you buy homes too?

Yes—SFRs that meet our buy box. We can also place assignments via our buyer network.

Are you a brokerage?

We operate as a private acquisitions company and may partner with licensed agents when appropriate.

What about flood/wetlands?

We review FEMA/state data. Mitigable scenarios may still qualify depending on access/comps.
